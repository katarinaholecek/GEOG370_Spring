In this project I learned how to display images in different projections

The first thing I did was download the base map from Nature Earth. Then, after opening it as a vector layer in QGIS I added an indicatrix mapper. Finally, for each projection I used different EPSG codes.

WGS84 Projection

As you get further from the equator the shae starts to get distorted.

Aitoff Projection

The area becomes distorted towards the outer areas of the map.

Pseudo Mercator Projection

Although the shape is kept constant, the area is largely thrown off.

Sphere Winkel Projection

The closer you get to the poles, the more distorted the shape.

World Cylindrical Equal Area Projection

The projections here are more condense but area and shape seem fine until you reach the poles.

World Equidistant Conic Projection

The area is distorted the closer you get to the North Pole.

North Pole Azimuthal Equidistant Projection

The area is also distorted the further you get from the North Pole.

Sphere Mollweide Projection

Area is preserved more than shape.

World Loximuthal Projection

The South Pole is further distorted than the North Pole.
